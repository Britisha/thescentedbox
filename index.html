The Scented Box: Our Story

As a small business born during the challenging times of the COVID-19 lockdown, our story is one of resilience and unwavering passion. We started with handmade soaps and quickly gained a loyal customer base who eagerly demanded our expansion into skincare. With their overwhelming support, we have been on a relentless pursuit of excellence. Through diligent research, continuous education, and rigorous product testing, we have developed high-quality skincare offerings that have captured the hearts of our customers. Innovation, learning, and pushing boundaries are at the core of our approach as we constantly strive to create new and exciting products. What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to using organically sourced ingredients and being completely cruelty-free, in line with the growing demand for ethical skincare. Furthermore, our customizable gift boxes add a personalized touch to our offerings, enhancing the overall customer experience. Join us on our journey and fall in love with our unique story and exceptional skincare products that are crafted with care for you and your skin.
